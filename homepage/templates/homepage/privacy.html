PRIVACY POLICY

We place a high value on maintaining your privacy. We believe that the following statements will help you understand how our app handles your personal information and other information.

Information we collect

1.) Personal Information: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the app. We collect phone numbers, country codes and usernames. We also collect information regarding push notifications when you use our app. We send you push notifications in the form of OTP (one time passwords) to verify the mobile numbers. We request access to your mobile device’s SMS messages so that you can successfully receive OTP. This information is primarily needed to maintain the operation of our app.
2.) Cookies and Ads: We do not send any cookies files to your mobile device and the application is Ad free.
3.) Health Kit and Wearables: You will have an option to link and import health data into the app from third party services like Google Fit, Fitbit, OuraRing etc.

How we use your information

1.) To facilitate account creation and logon process.
2.) We use your information to manage your account and keep it in working order.
3.) We collect your phone number and country code to send the OTP to your device.
4.) We may use your information in order to enable user to user communications.
5.) Your Health kit and wearable credentials will not be stored anywhere.
6.) You can review the information we collect from wearables on the page where you allow access.

Will your information be shared with anyone?

If you interact with other users of our app, only then your information will be shared to them.

What are your privacy rights?

You may review, change or terminate your account at any time.
