PRIVACY POLICY

We place a high value on maintaining your privacy. We believe that the following statements will help you understand how we handle your personal information and other information.

Information we collect

1.) Personal Information: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website. We only take your name while you register on the website.
2.) Cookies and Ads: We do not show any ads on our website. Cookies are only used for the registration process which requires those recognise the user as registration of the user and their wearable account is a multiple step process.
3.) Health Kit and Wearables: On Registering you will give us the permission to read your health and fitness data. We dont modify or alter your data. At any time you can deny access to our app from your wearable website or app.

How we use your information

1.) To facilitate registration process.
2.) We use your information to for study purposes only.
3.) Your wearable login credentials will not be stored anywhere.
4.) You can review the information we collect from wearables on the page where you allow access.

Will your information be shared with anyone?

Your information will not be shared with anyone and will be kept securely

What are your privacy rights?

You may terminate your account at any time from the website of the wearable you linked with us.
